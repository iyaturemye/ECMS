About us

The ECMS is an idea born from Mr Karambizi Innocent, having more than twenty years’ experience in automobile e cervices. His observations in car maintenance and its role in hampering the insurance economic market, brought the gap to the study on how to recover losses in insurance companies and break the corruption system in car maintenances. He therefore introduced the Electronic Claims management system, as a solution structure through Kigali Integrated College researchers. The research result links general automobile maintenance, accident insurance claims and garages sector to eradicate the triangle fraudulent behavior among insurance companies, owners, and automobile garages. The above said system shall help users from loss recovery, open competition, accountability, transparency and to eradicate monopolistic market hence the economic development.
